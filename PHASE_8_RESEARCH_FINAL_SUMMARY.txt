  Update system to use ฿1,500 for OFC+LGO insurance group

Effort: 1-2 hours
Priority: HIGH


FINDING 2: DRUG REIMBURSEMENT - MOSTLY CORRECT ✅ (with ONE ISSUE)
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Current System:
  All drugs: 40% of retail price

NHSO Official:
  Generic drugs:          40% ✅ CORRECT
  Brand drugs:            40-45% ✅ ACCEPTABLE
  Dialysis-special drugs: 50-60% ❌ CURRENTLY WRONG (using 40%)

Dialysis-Special Drugs (need higher reimbursement):
  - EPO (Erythropoietin) - treats anemia
  - Iron supplements - supports EPO
  - Vitamin D analogs - bone health
  - Phosphate binders - mineral management

Impact:
  Dialysis drug reimbursement is too low
  - Example (EPO): Getting ฿200/dose instead of ฿250-300
  - Per patient: +฿3,750-5,000/year when corrected
  - For hospital (100 patients): +฿3,750-5,000/year

Action Required:
  Create special category for dialysis drugs at 50-60% reimbursement

Effort: 2-3 hours
Priority: HIGH
